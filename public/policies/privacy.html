LIBREROOMS.ORG PRIVACY POLICY Glossary: PII - Personally identifiable information LibreRooms, site, app, we, us - librerooms.org This website does not track its users or store, collect or process personally identifiable information. What we collect: • Login information (usernames and passwords) In some cases passwords may be considered PII, but here they are not because the accounts they give access to do not contain PII. While accounts do not contain sensitive or otherwise private information, we still have measures to prevent account theft such as password hashing. • Messages We do not knowingly collect personal information and have a Terms of Service in place that disallows users to share personal information through messages or otherwise. • Account data We may also collect data which is stored for in-app features and functionality, such as avatars, user descriptions and user preferences.
